Wopower

About us

WoPower is a non-profit organization and our major objective is to support African women from abusive relationships. Our goal is to teach, empower and embrace our womanhood, We empower girls/women to become catalysts for change and bring about gender equality.

Our Projects

Southern Africa Region

Women In Tech

This is a program focussed on bridging the gender gap in the tech industry. Our organisation conducts a bi-monthly bootcamp to teach women tech skills.

Eastern Africa Region

Stand To End Sexual Abuse

Over 35% of women in the world has suffered sexual violence with their intimate partner. 90% of adult rape victims are women. Our organisation works to decrease these statisties by education people on rape and sexual violence.

Western Africa Region

Clean The City

Our organization understands the importance of a clean city and the benefits of a good environment. This bi-weekly project is an opportunity to improve the quality of our collective lives.

Northern Africa Region

Plant Trees

Climate change has been a universal concern in recent years and the world is harshly seeing the consequemces of our action. This project focuses to improve the state of our planet by planting trees in every city and town.

FAQ

Do i need to pay?

You do not need to make any payments and you do
not need any money. We are a free organisation
out to help the women folk.

Is this service available all over the world?

No, we are only available in Nigeria,
Ghana, Kenya and South Africa. We have officers in all these countries
who are well trained to be at your service should you need any help.

Can i get legal aid?

Yes, we have a lawyer who is available
and ready to take up any case regarding domestic violence
and at a free-course.
